# 🌌 AstroLogic AI

AstroLogic AI is a high-fidelity, full-stack astrology prediction platform that merges ancient Indian wisdom with modern Artificial Intelligence. By combining Vedic Astrology and Numerology (Moolank & Bhagyank), it provides users with personalized insights into their personality, career, love life, and areas for growth.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 1. Real-time AI Predictions

Get instant, human-friendly insights generated by the Google Gemini Pro model. No generic horoscopes—every reading is tailored to your specific birth details.

### 2. Vedic & Numerology Engine

The system internally calculates your Moolank (Root Number) and Bhagyank (Destiny Number) to provide deeper psychological and karmic insights without complex jargon.

### 3. Session History 📜

Never lose a reading. Our new History Sidebar allows you to revisit previous predictions during your current session. You can click on any past record to instantly re-populate the form.

### 4. Downloadable Reports 📥

Users can export their celestial predictions as high-quality PNG images. This feature uses html2canvas to capture the beautifully styled result cards for easy sharing on social media.

### 5. Cosmic UI/UX

* **Adaptive Theme:** Seamlessly switch between Light and Deep Space (Dark) modes.
* **Interactive Background:** A dynamic, floating starfield canvas that responds to theme changes.
* **Mobile-First Design:** Fully responsive layout built with Tailwind CSS.

---

## 🤖 AI Integration

This project leverages the Google Gemini 1.5 Flash API to act as a "Digital Pandit."

* **Prompt Engineering:** The backend uses a strictly structured system prompt that enforces a warm, professional Indian astrological tone while ensuring the output is always valid JSON for the frontend to render.
* **Secure API Handling:** The API key is managed via environment variables (.env) to ensure security during local development and production deployment.

---

## 🔢 Calculation Logic

The AI is trained to interpret real-time data points:

* **Moolank (Root Number):** Derived from your birth date, representing your basic nature and core personality.
* **Bhagyank (Destiny Number):** Calculated from your full date of birth, representing the path you are destined to follow.
* **Rashi-Based Insights:** Integrates the position of the Sun and Moon based on your birth time and location to define emotional and professional tendencies.

## 📸 Step-by-Step Walkthrough

### Level 1: The Cosmic Entrance

Users land on a clean, celestial-themed form. The Zodiac Header updates in real-time as you enter your Date of Birth, showing your Sun Sign icon and its Hindi name.

### Level 2: The Consultation

Upon clicking "Unlock My Stars," a cosmic loading animation appears. The backend sends your details to the Gemini API, which performs the Vedic and Numerological calculations in seconds.

### Level 3: The Revelation

Predictions are displayed in four distinct, glassmorphism-styled cards:

* **Personality:** Your core traits and strengths.
* **Career:** Work style and professional advice.
* **Love:** Emotional nature and relationship strengths.
* **Improvement:** Practical shifts for a better future.

### Level 4: Archiving & Exporting

Users can view their Session History in the sidebar or hit the Download button to save a 3x scaled, high-resolution snapshot of their results to their device.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** HTML5, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript (ES6+), Flatpickr, html2canvas
* **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
* **AI:** Google Generative AI (Gemini API)
* **Deployment:** Netlify (using Serverless Functions)

---

## 🛠️ Setup Instructions

1. Clone the repo.
2. Create a `.env` file based on `.env.example` and add your `GEMINI_API_KEY`.
3. Run `npm install`.
4. Launch locally with `node server.js` or deploy to Netlify.
